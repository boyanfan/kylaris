# Kylaris

Kylaris is an AI-driven trading system that combines 
data analytics and generative models to provide 
intelligent trading insights and strategies across 
multiple markets. 

---

Kylaris is a name born from the fusion of _Kyla_ and 
_Aris_, derived from the Greek, meaning _“the best”_ or 
_“excellence”_. Together, Kylaris embodies the idea of 
clarity perfected through wisdom - an intelligence 
that sees patterns beneath noise, acts with calm precision, 
and evolves toward mastery.
